## About Hack Club Mobile

The **Hack Club Mobile App** is designed to bring the [Hack Club](https://hackclub.com) experience directly to your smartphone. It allows members to easily access Hack Club resources, stay up-to-date with events, collaborate with fellow coders, and participate in club activities on the go.

With a focus on community and learning, the app makes coding more accessible and fun for students worldwide. You can take on coding challenges, explore project ideas, view your [Hackatime](https://hackatime.hackclub.com) stats, connect with other hackers, and discover [YSWS](https://ysws.hackclub.com) projects (“You Ship, We Ship”) to see what others are building and share your own creations. The **Hack Club Mobile App** puts everything you need to code, collaborate, and showcase your work right at your fingertips.
